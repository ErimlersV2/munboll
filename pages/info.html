What is Model United Nations or MUN?

Model United Nations, also known as Model UN or MUN, is an extra-curricular activity in which students typically roleplay delegates to the United Nations and simulate UN committees. This activity takes place at MUN conferecences, which is usually organized by a high school or an universities MUN club.
